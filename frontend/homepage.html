About UTRGV

University of Texas Rio Grande Valley

Welcome to UTRGV

The University of Texas Rio Grande Valley (UTRGV) was created by the Texas Legislature in 2013 as the first major public university of the 21st century in Texas. This transformational initiative provided the opportunity to expand educational opportunities in the Rio Grande Valley and beyond.

Mission

UTRGV is a comprehensive academic institution with a strong focus on student success, research, and community engagement. We are committed to providing accessible, affordable, and high-quality education to our diverse student population while advancing knowledge through research and creative works.

Campuses

UTRGV serves students across two primary campuses in Edinburg and Brownsville, Texas. With state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated faculty, we offer over 170 degree programs at the undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral levels.

Student Life

Our vibrant campus community offers numerous opportunities for students to get involved through clubs, organizations, athletic events, and university traditions. UTRGV is home to the Vaqueros, competing in NCAA Division I athletics, and hosts a variety of cultural, academic, and social events throughout the year.

Streamio at UTRGV

Streamio connects the UTRGV community by providing a platform to stream live events, club activities, sports games, academic presentations, and more. Stay connected with what's happening across both campuses in real-time and never miss an important moment in Vaquero life!
